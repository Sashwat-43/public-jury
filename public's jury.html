PUBLIC'S JURY is an online platform created to operate at Municipal Level where the public would be able to register complaints,write reviews or give information regarding the problems in their locality.The problems may be regarding pot holes,non-functioning of street lights,pipe leakage or any such problem which needs to be seek attention of the concerned officials.The user just needs to login/signup first with some of his details so as to ensure the credibility of the complaints registered by the users.
